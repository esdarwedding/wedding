DRESS CODE

We kindly invite our guests to join us in celebrating in Black Tie attire.

For gentlemen, this typically means a tuxedo or a dark formal suit with a tie or bow tie. For ladies, a long evening gown or elegant formal attire would be perfect.

THE MENU

We both really enjoy good food, so choosing the menu was something we were especially excited about. We have selected mains that we personally love and hope you will enjoy as much as we do.

Three options are provided for the main courses: Norwegian Salmon or Slow Roasted Pork Belly. For vegetarians, we have a Phyllo Pocket.

Please contact us if you have any allergies or other dietary requirements.

FAQ

When should I arrive?

Guest arrival time from 14:30. All guest to be seated by 14:45.

Is parking available?

Yes. There is sufficient parking available at the venue, and guests will be able to park on-site.

Is transport provided?

Guests are required to arrange their own transport to the venue. Uber is not readily available, however some local shuttle services are provided by Dtrack Tours (083 377 6614) and Zane (082 045 1754).

Is the accommodation self-catering?

Yes. The accommodation is self-catering, so guests staying on-site should please plan accordingly.

Are children welcome?

While we love your little ones, unfortunately children are not invited to the wedding. If needed, we can put parents in touch with each other to help coordinate childcare arrangements.

Does the invitation include a plus one?

This invitation is extended to you and those listed on your invitation only. Unfortunatley no additional plus ones can be accomodated.

Will drinks be available for purchase?

There will be a cash bar stocked with drinks available for purchase on the night. The bar will be card only, unfortunately the venue does not accept cash.
